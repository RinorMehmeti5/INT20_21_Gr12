Spring in Albania

Spring in Albania lasts from mid-February until mid-April. The weather in February is mostly rainy. In early March, the weather changes to warm and sunny. is established and everything starts to blossom and everything starts to blossom. At this time of year, the color diversity of Albanian flora becomes extraordinarily attractive

Summer in Albania

Summer months bring intense heat and drought. At this time of the year the sky is usually very clear, high and deep blue. Rains are almost non-existent except for some mountain areas.

Autumn in Albania

Autumns in Albania are quite short. Autumn usually it begins in late October, and lasts just a little more than a month. During this period, it often rains, although the average temperature is kept at around +18ºC, but the sea is already too cold for going to the beach.

Winter in Albania

Winters in Albania are are freezing and humid. It snows and rains in different parts of Albania. The coldest month is January, its average daily temperature ranges from +5 to +10ºC. At the same time, the temperature regime strongly depends on the altitude of the place above sea level, therefore, in some mountainous regions the temperature can drop to -12 - -20ºC.
